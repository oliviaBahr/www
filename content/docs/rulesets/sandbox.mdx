## Overview

The Sandbox ruleset is designed for testing and experimentation with balance changes and new mechanics before they are considered for the Standard ruleset. It serves as a proving ground for card modifications, new interactions, and gameplay adjustments.

Based on the Standard ruleset, with the following changes:

## Gameplay Changes

- **Comeback Gold Mechanic:** Changed from $4 per life lost to $3 per completed Ante
- **The Order:** Re-enabled in the joker pool with modified effect
- **Score Preview:** Disabled
- **Justice Tarot Card:** Re-enabled (Glass cards break at 1/3 rate)

## Rationale

The Sandbox ruleset serves as a testing ground for potential balance changes before they're considered for Standard play. Changes focus on:

- **Increased build diversity:** Modifications to underused jokers to make them more competitive
- **Risk/reward balance:** Adjustments to cards with high variance or inconsistent effects
- **Consistency improvements:** Removing RNG elements that change every round (Castle, Mail-in Rebate)
- **Mechanical experimentation:** Testing new interactions (Steel Joker retriggers, Vampire stone conversion)
- **Power level adjustments:** Buffing weak cards while adding restrictions to powerful ones

Changes are regularly rotated and refined based on playtesting feedback. Successful modifications may eventually be promoted to the Standard ruleset.
